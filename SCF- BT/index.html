Welcome To Susrusha Cancer Foundation

Sushrusa cancer foundation is a healthcare platform built with a vision of making the cancer care available to the poorest and underprivileged sections of the community.

The foundation is covered under Sec 80 G and is exempt from taxes. Anyone can contribute in their convenient way to help the foundation achieve its goal and mission.
